About the Role

We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Product Designer to spearhead the evolution of our institutional FinTech platform. You will be responsible for translating complex data structures into intuitive, high-performance dashboard experiences that serve thousands of financial analysts daily.

Core Responsibilities

  • Conduct comprehensive user research with stakeholders in the financial services sector.
  • Develop high-fidelity prototypes using Figma that demonstrate complex data visualization techniques.
  • Collaborate directly with our engineering team to ensure design feasibility and pixel-perfect implementation.
  • Establish and maintain a robust design system that scales across multiple sub-products.

Requirements

  • 8+ years of experience in Product Design, with at least 3 years in the FinTech or Enterprise SaaS space.
  • Mastery of Figma, including advanced prototyping and auto-layout features.
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating successful complex dashboard projects.
  • Excellent communication skills for cross-functional collaboration.
